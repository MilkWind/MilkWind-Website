<p class="text-base leading-7 mb-4 text-foreground"><strong class="font-bold text-foreground">Why does the starfield need useMemo</strong>?</p>
<p class="text-base leading-7 mb-4 text-foreground">Star generation involves 20 random calculations and DOM element creation. If regenerated on every component render, it would cause:</p>
<ol class="list-decimal list-inside mb-4 pl-6 space-y-2">
<li class="text-foreground leading-relaxed"><strong class="font-bold text-foreground">Performance issues</strong>: Repeated random calculations and element creation</li>
<li class="text-foreground leading-relaxed"><strong class="font-bold text-foreground">Visual issues</strong>: Constantly changing star positions, breaking the static background effect</li>
<li class="text-foreground leading-relaxed"><strong class="font-bold text-foreground">Animation issues</strong>: Recreated stars would restart animations, causing flickering</li>
</ol>
<p class="text-base leading-7 mb-4 text-foreground">Through <code>useMemo</code>, we ensure the starfield is only generated once during the component’s first render, then remains stable.</p>
