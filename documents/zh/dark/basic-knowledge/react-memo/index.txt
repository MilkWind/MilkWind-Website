<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-4 pl-6 space-y-2">
<li class="text-foreground leading-relaxed">组件频繁渲染，但 props 变化较少。</li>
<li class="text-foreground leading-relaxed">组件树较大，优化渲染性能。</li>
<li class="text-foreground leading-relaxed">静态展示组件（如列表项、卡片）。</li>
</ul>
